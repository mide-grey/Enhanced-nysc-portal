Features

Everything you need
in one smart portal

Designed specifically for Nigeria's National Youth Service Corps members and prospective corpers.

⚡
Instant Answers
Get immediate responses to your NYSC questions without waiting in long queues or browsing outdated FAQs. Real-time AI-powered support.
🎙️
Voice Interaction
Speak your questions naturally using the microphone feature. Perfect for when you're on the go or prefer talking over typing.
🌍
Multilingual Support
Communicate in English, Yoruba, or Hausa. The AI understands and responds in your preferred language for true inclusivity.
📋
NYSC Document Help
Get guidance on call-up letters, exclusion letters, relocation requests, and all essential documentation required throughout service year.
